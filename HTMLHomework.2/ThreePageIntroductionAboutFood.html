"Husband and wife lung tablets"

"Husband and wife lung film" is its Chinese name, and its English name is "Smiths". It was invented by a couple. It is made of beef scalp, beef heart, beef tongue, tripe, and beef as the main ingredients, marinated and then sliced. Add chili oil, pepper noodles and other auxiliary materials to make red oil and pour it on top.
